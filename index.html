About Me

Hello! I'm Madeline Metzler, a fourth-year student in the B.arch program at NYIT. Through interning for engineers/architects in high school, I developed a deep understanding of architecture's power to impact communities. My frequent relocations across America provided firsthand insight into how the built environment shapes human experience. This has driven my focus on innovative, sustainable design for underserved areas. I aim to work on diverse projects that promote community connection while prioritizing environmental conservation.
